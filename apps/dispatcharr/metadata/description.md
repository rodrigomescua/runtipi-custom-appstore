# Dispatcharr

Your Ultimate IPTV & Stream Management Companion

Dispatcharr is a comprehensive IPTV and stream management platform that gives you complete control over your streaming infrastructure. Whether you're managing IPTV services, VOD content, or live streams, Dispatcharr provides the tools you need.

## Features

- **IPTV Stream Management**: Organize and manage your IPTV streams with ease
- **EPG Integration**: Seamlessly integrate Electronic Program Guides
- **VOD Support**: Manage Video-On-Demand content with automatic categorization
- **Proxy Streaming**: Route streams through Dispatcharr for enhanced control and monitoring
- **M3U/EPG Endpoints**: Create and manage M3U and EPG endpoints with caching and validation
- **System Event Logging**: Track system events and monitor application health
- **Authentication**: Secure your streams with built-in authentication
- **Advanced Statistics**: Monitor usage and performance with detailed stats

## Getting Started

1. Access the application at http://localhost:8843
2. Configure your IPTV sources and EPG providers
3. Create M3U/EPG endpoints for your clients
4. Start streaming!
