# CondenseIt

CondenseIt is a self-hosted, AI-assisted news digest generator designed to aggregate your daily reading from various sources and summarize it using LLMs.

## Features

- **Multi-Source Aggregation:** Collect feeds from RSS/Atom, YouTube transcripts, Reddit, Hacker News, GitHub Releases, Google News, and even website change detection.
- **AI Summarization:** Summarize content using local models via Ollama or remote options like OpenRouter and OpenAI.
- **Smart Re-ranking:** A personalized ranking engine that learns your preferences based on explicit reading signals, likes, and skips.
- **Self-Hosted & Private:** Keep your data on your own hardware, running seamlessly under your control.
